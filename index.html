About the Project

NeuralRX is an initiative born from the prestigious Smart India Hackathon, with a mission to revolutionize healthcare accessibility in Odisha. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with medical expertise to provide reliable health guidance to communities across the state.

Developed in collaboration with healthcare professionals and technology experts, our system is designed to be a trusted first point of contact for health concerns, helping users make informed decisions about their wellbeing.

This project is proudly supported by the Government of Odisha as part of its digital health initiatives to improve public health outcomes through technology innovation.
